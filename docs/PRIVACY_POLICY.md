# JIRAForge Privacy Policy



---

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how JIRAForge ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, shares, and protects information when you use our Jira time tracking application ("Forge App") and companion desktop application ("Desktop App"), collectively referred to as the "Services."

By using our Services, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our practices, please do not use our Services.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide, maintain, and improve our Services. The types of information we collect depend on how you use our Services.

### 1.1 Information You Provide to Us

**Account Information:**
- Name and email address (from your Atlassian account)
- Organization/workspace information
- User preferences and settings

**Time Tracking Data:**
- Time entries (start time, end time, duration)
- Project and task associations
- Work descriptions and notes
- Break periods and idle time records

### 1.2 Information Collected by the Desktop App

When you install and use the JIRAForge Desktop App, we may collect the following information while you are actively tracking time:

**Screenshot Data:**
- Periodic screenshots of your desktop screen (frequency configurable by your administrator)
- Screenshots are captured only when time tracking is active
- You will receive a notification when screenshot capture is enabled

**Activity Data:**
- Names and titles of active applications and windows
- Time spent in each application
- Active vs. idle time detection
- Keyboard and mouse activity levels (activity counts only - **we do NOT record keystrokes or what you type**)

**Device Information:**
- Operating system type and version
- Device identifiers
- IP address
- Application version

> **Important:** JIRAForge is NOT a keylogger. We only detect whether keyboard/mouse activity is occurring, not the content of what you type. We do not capture passwords, personal messages, or any typed content.

### 1.3 Information Collected Automatically

**Log Data:**
- Access times and dates
- Features used within the Services
- Error logs and performance data
- Browser type (for web-based features)

**Cookies and Similar Technologies:**
- Session cookies for authentication
- Preference cookies for settings
- Analytics cookies (with consent)

For more details, see our [Cookie Notice](#cookie-notice).

### 1.4 Information from Third Parties

**Atlassian/Jira:**
- Jira project and issue information
- User profile data from your Atlassian account
- Workspace and team membership information

- **Encryption in Transit:** All data transmitted using TLS 1.2+ (HTTPS)

### 5.2 Organizational Safeguards

- Regular security assessments and audits
- Employee training on data protection
- Incident response procedures
- Vendor security reviews

### 5.3 Your Responsibilities

Despite our efforts, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 10. Desktop App Specific Disclosures

The JIRAForge Desktop App has additional data collection capabilities that require specific disclosure:

### 10.1 Screenshot Capture

- **When Captured:** Only while time tracking is actively running
- **Frequency:** Configurable by your administrator (default: every 10 minutes)
- **Notification:** You will see a visual indicator when screenshot capture is enabled
- **What's Captured:** Your entire desktop screen at the moment of capture
- **Storage:** Screenshots are uploaded securely to encrypted cloud storage
- **Access:** Visible to you, your designated managers, and administrators
- **Deletion:** Automatically deleted after retention period (default: 90 days)

### 10.2 Application/Window Tracking

- **What's Tracked:** Names and titles of active applications and browser windows
- **Purpose:** Categorizing work activities and generating productivity reports
- **Privacy Consideration:** Window titles may contain sensitive information (e.g., document names, email subjects)

### 10.3 Idle Detection

- **How It Works:** Monitors keyboard and mouse activity to detect idle periods
- **Threshold:** Configurable idle timeout (default: 5 minutes of inactivity)
- **Purpose:** Accurate time tracking and automatic break detection
- **Privacy Note:** We detect activity presence only, NOT the content of your actions

### 10.4 Background Operation

- **When Running:** The Desktop App may run in the background while tracking time
- **System Tray:** Accessible via system tray icon
- **Resource Usage:** Minimal CPU and memory footprint
- **Auto-Start:** Configurable option to start with your operating system

### 10.5 Opting Out

You may:
- Disable screenshot capture (if permitted by your administrator)
- Pause or stop time tracking at any time
- Uninstall the Desktop App
- Request data deletion through your account settings
