1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide, maintain, and improve our Services. The types of information we collect depend on how you use our Services.

1.1 Information You Provide to Us

Account Information:

  • Name and email address (from your Atlassian account)
  • Organization/workspace information
  • User preferences and settings

Time Tracking Data:

  • Time entries (start time, end time, duration)
  • Project and task associations
  • Work descriptions and notes
  • Break periods and idle time records

1.2 Information Collected by the Desktop App

When you install and use the JIRAForge Desktop App, we may collect the following information while you are actively tracking time:

Screenshot Data:

  • Periodic screenshots of your desktop screen (frequency configurable by your administrator)
  • Screenshots are captured only when time tracking is active
  • You will receive a notification when screenshot capture is enabled

Activity Data:

  • Names and titles of active applications and windows
  • Time spent in each application
  • Active vs. idle time detection
  • Keyboard and mouse activity levels (activity counts only)
⚠️ Important: We Are NOT a Keylogger

JIRAForge only detects whether keyboard/mouse activity is occurring, not the content of what you type. We do not capture passwords, personal messages, or any typed content.

Device Information:

  • Operating system type and version
  • Device identifiers
  • IP address
  • Application version

1.3 Information Collected Automatically

  • Access times and dates
  • Features used within the Services
  • Error logs and performance data
  • Browser type (for web-based features)
  • Cookies and similar technologies

1.4 Information from Third Parties

We receive information from Atlassian/Jira including:

  • Jira project and issue information
  • User profile data from your Atlassian account
  • Workspace and team membership information

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Providing and Operating the Services

  • Tracking and recording work time
  • Generating timesheets and reports
  • Syncing time entries with Jira issues
  • Providing productivity insights and analytics
  • Processing idle time and break management

2.2 AI-Powered Features

We use artificial intelligence to provide enhanced features, including:

  • Smart Time Suggestions: Analyzing work patterns to suggest time entries
  • Activity Categorization: Automatically categorizing work activities
  • Screenshot Analysis: Using AI to extract context from screenshots (optional feature)
ℹ️ Third-Party AI Processing

Some AI features utilize OpenAI's services. When enabled, screenshot images may be processed by OpenAI for text extraction and analysis. Data sent to OpenAI is subject to OpenAI's Privacy Policy. You can disable AI-powered features in your settings.

2.3 Security and Fraud Prevention

  • Authenticating users and verifying access
  • Detecting and preventing unauthorized access
  • Monitoring for security threats
  • Enforcing our Terms of Service

2.4 Improving Our Services

  • Analyzing usage patterns to improve features
  • Debugging and fixing issues
  • Developing new functionality
  • Conducting research and analytics

2.5 Communications

  • Sending service-related notifications
  • Responding to support requests
  • Providing product updates (with consent)

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only in the following circumstances:

If you use JIRAForge through an employer or organization:

  • Team members may see aggregated project time data

Your organization controls the data collected about you through their use of our Services.

Provider Purpose Location

These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for the purposes we specify.

3.3 For Legal Reasons

We may disclose your information if required to:

  • Comply with applicable laws, regulations, or legal processes
  • Respond to lawful requests from public authorities
  • Protect our rights, property, or safety
  • Enforce our Terms of Service
  • Detect and prevent fraud or security issues

3.4 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the acquiring entity, subject to the same privacy protections.

3.5 With Your Consent

We may share your information with third parties when you explicitly consent to such sharing.

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our Services and fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

5.1 Technical Safeguards

  • Encryption in Transit: All data transmitted using TLS 1.2+ (HTTPS)
  • Encryption at Rest: Stored data encrypted using AES-256
  • Authentication: OAuth 2.0 with PKCE for secure login
  • Access Controls: Role-based access to limit data exposure
  • Secure Storage: Screenshots and files stored in encrypted cloud storage

5.2 Organizational Safeguards

  • Regular security assessments and audits
  • Employee training on data protection
  • Incident response procedures
  • Vendor security reviews

5.3 Your Responsibilities

  • Keep your login credentials secure
  • Log out of shared devices
  • Report any suspected security incidents to us immediately

Despite our efforts, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Desktop App Specific Disclosures

The JIRAForge Desktop App has additional data collection capabilities that require specific disclosure:

10.1 Screenshot Capture

When Captured Only while time tracking is actively running
Frequency Configurable by your administrator (default: every 10 minutes)
Notification You will see a visual indicator when screenshot capture is enabled
What's Captured Your entire desktop screen at the moment of capture
Storage Screenshots are uploaded securely to encrypted cloud storage
Access Visible to you, your designated managers, and administrators
Deletion Automatically deleted after retention period (default: 90 days)

10.2 Application/Window Tracking

  • What's Tracked: Names and titles of active applications and browser windows
  • Purpose: Categorizing work activities and generating productivity reports
  • Privacy Consideration: Window titles may contain sensitive information (e.g., document names, email subjects)

10.3 Idle Detection

  • How It Works: Monitors keyboard and mouse activity to detect idle periods
  • Threshold: Configurable idle timeout (default: 5 minutes of inactivity)
  • Purpose: Accurate time tracking and automatic break detection
  • Privacy Note: We detect activity presence only, NOT the content of your actions

10.4 Background Operation

  • When Running: The Desktop App may run in the background while tracking time
  • System Tray: Accessible via system tray icon
  • Resource Usage: Minimal CPU and memory footprint
  • Auto-Start: Configurable option to start with your operating system

10.5 Opting Out

You may:

  • Disable screenshot capture (if permitted by your administrator)
  • Pause or stop time tracking at any time
  • Uninstall the Desktop App
  • Request data deletion through your account settings
